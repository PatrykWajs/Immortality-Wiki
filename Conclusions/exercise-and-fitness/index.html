    EP-13 — My posture nearly killed me. Here's why...¶

    Many life-threatening conditions remain undetected by standard medical care because conventional annual physicals don't include advanced vascular and neurological screening. Poor posture is not merely a cosmetic or musculoskeletal issue—it directly affects cerebral blood flow and intracranial pressure, creating potential for serious neurological events. The gap between standard care and comprehensive preventative testing is substantial enough that most people may be living with undiagnosed vascular conditions. Posture optimization should be treated as a critical longevity intervention alongside other health optimization protocols.
