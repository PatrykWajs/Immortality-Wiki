    The term “superfood” has entered the popular vernacular in the last few decades, signifying foods that are nutrient-rich and beneficial for health and well-being.     Nuts and seeds are excellent sources of heart-healthy fats, protein, and fiber. They also possess anti-inflammatory properties and can help manage weight[^5^].
