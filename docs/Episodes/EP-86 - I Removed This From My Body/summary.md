## Core Concepts

**Total Plasma Exchange (TPE)**
A medical procedure that removes the liquid component of blood (plasma) containing accumulated waste products and toxins, replacing it with clean albumin. The theory posits that removing this "dirty" plasma and replacing it with clean solution allows the body to rebuild healthier plasma while eliminating accumulated metabolic waste.

**Plasma Quality & Biomarker Measurement**
The cleanliness of plasma reflects lifestyle adherence—sleep quality, diet, exercise, and toxin avoidance directly influence plasma composition. Blueprint methodology involves measuring dozens of biomarkers pre- and post-intervention to quantify biological age changes.

**Intergenerational Plasma Therapy**
Plasma from a younger or biologically optimized individual can be transfused to older individuals, potentially reversing aging markers and improving vitality—evidenced by Bryan's father's 25-year aging speed reduction.
