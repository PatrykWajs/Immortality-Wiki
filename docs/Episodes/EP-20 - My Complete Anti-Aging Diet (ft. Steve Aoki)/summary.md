## Core Concepts

**Blueprint Protocol** — Bryan Johnson's comprehensive anti-aging system that emphasizes nutrient density, bioavailability, and elimination of empty calories. Every ingredient selection is evidence-based and targeted toward specific health outcomes.

**Green Giant** — A consolidated supplement beverage designed to replace 60+ individual pills while maintaining efficacy. Contains spermidine (longevity compound), amino acids, creatine, collagen, and cocoa flavonols for comprehensive cellular support.

**Super Veggie v1 & v2** — A vegetable-forward meal designed for sustained nutrient intake. The puree version (v2) with olive oil and dark chocolate added improves palatability and adherence, making consistent vegetable consumption practical.

**Intermittent Fasting Window** — A 4-hour eating window (7 AM–11 AM) followed by 10+ hours fasting to optimize sleep quality, though noted as flexible based on individual preference and lifestyle.

**Polyphenol Optimization** — Emphasis on sourcing the highest quality versions of foods (especially olive oil and dark chocolate) through testing for heavy metals and verifying polyphenol content rather than accepting standard commercial versions.

## Practical Takeaways

1. **Consolidate supplementation:** Create a morning drink combining key longevity compounds (spermidine, creatine, collagen, polyphenols) to replace multiple pills and improve daily compliance.

2. **Make vegetables palatable:** If standard vegetable consumption is difficult, blend them into a puree and add high-quality olive oil and dark chocolate to increase enjoyment without sacrificing nutritional benefit.

3. **Implement an eating window:** Adopt a 4-hour concentrated eating period followed by extended fasting if sleep optimization is a priority; adjust timing based on your schedule and hunger patterns.

4. **Audit your pantry:** Remove processed snacks and replace with whole-food options where every calorie serves a nutritional purpose—this removes friction from poor food choices.

5. **Prioritize ingredient quality:** For staple foods like olive oil and dark chocolate, research sourcing, heavy metal testing, and polyphenol content rather than purchasing conventionally available versions.

6. **Customize to your lifestyle:** Use Bryan's protocol as a template but adjust meal timing and composition based on your own circadian rhythm, travel schedule, and hunger preferences (like Steve does).

7. **Access free resources:** Utilize the publicly available Blueprint Protocol recipes and supplement lists online to start with baby steps rather than attempting complete protocol adoption immediately.
