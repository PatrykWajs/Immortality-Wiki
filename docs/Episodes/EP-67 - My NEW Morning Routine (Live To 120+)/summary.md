## Practical Takeaways

1. **Map your morning routine**: Structure the first 2 hours into a repeatable sequence (light exposure, temperature check, weigh-in, supplements, breakfast) so it becomes automatic rather than requiring daily decisions.

2. **Test your supplements**: Request third-party lab reports for any supplement you consume; don't trust label claims. If a company won't provide data, find alternatives.

3. **Prioritize fiber intake**: Aim for 30g+ daily through vegetables, seeds, and legumes. Start by replacing processed foods incrementally rather than overhauling diet overnight.

4. **Invest in sleep environment optimization**: Priority order is mattress temperature control (8Sleep or similar), humidity (stainless steel humidifier), grounding sheets, and noise masking.

5. **Start hair care early**: Don't wait for visible loss. Implement scalp stimulation (massage, targeted serums) and red light therapy in your 20s-30s if family history suggests hair loss risk.

6. **Drink filtered water**: Implement basic water filtration; tap water can contain microplastics and contaminants even in developed regions like Los Angeles.

7. **Add 5 minutes of daily meditation**: Use future self visualization (imagining yourself decades ahead looking back) to gain perspective and reduce emotional reactivity to daily stressors.
