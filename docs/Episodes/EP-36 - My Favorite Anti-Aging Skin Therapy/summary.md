## Core Concepts

**Tixel Therapy (Thermomechanical Ablation)** — A fractional skin treatment using heated titanium spikes to create micro-channels in the skin at controlled depths (100-1,000 micrometers), reaching the dermal layers without requiring anesthesia.

**Exosomes** — Extracellular vesicles derived from stem cells (bone marrow and umbilical cord) that deliver anti-aging signals and growth factors through the micro-channels created by Tixel, potentially accelerating collagen remodeling and skin repair.

**1927 Nanometer Laser** — A specific wavelength laser used for drug-assisted delivery to the scalp, creating channels for exosome penetration to stimulate hair growth without unwanted hair removal.

**Recovery Time Reduction** — Exosome delivery can halve recovery time compared to traditional treatments, making the therapy more practical for ongoing use.
