## Core Concepts

**Iterative Diet Optimization**
Bryan applies software development methodology (version 1.0 → 2.0 → 3.0) to nutrition, continuously measuring biomarkers and removing ineffective elements while adding evidence-based improvements.

**Clinically Relevant Dosing**
Ingredients must be included at the exact dosages proven effective in peer-reviewed scientific studies, not arbitrary amounts. This distinguishes his approach from many supplement brands lacking dose transparency.

**Whole Food Powders**
Ground whole foods retain the nutritional value of their whole food counterparts while offering convenience and consistency. Real food powders are not "processed" in a harmful sense.

**Measurement-Driven Personalization**
Continuous biomarker testing allows for data-driven decisions about which interventions actually produce individual results, enabling customization beyond one-size-fits-all approaches.

**High-Grade Standards**
Premium ingredients (like extra virgin olive oil meeting six quality criteria) are essential; lower-quality versions may not deliver expected biological effects.
