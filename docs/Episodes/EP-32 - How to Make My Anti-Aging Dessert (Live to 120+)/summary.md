## Practical Takeaways

1. **Prepare Nutty Pudding at home** following the 7-minute protocol: combine all base ingredients in a blender with 50-100 mL nut milk, blend for 2 minutes until foamy
2. **Select berries strategically** — choose from strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, or blueberries based on availability and personal preference
3. **Customize nut milk consistency** by adjusting volume (50 mL for thicker pudding, 100 mL for thinner)
4. **Add protein strategically** by incorporating 30-60g of pea protein to support muscle maintenance and satiety
5. **Use toppings as functional layers** — chia seeds, pea protein, and fresh berries on top increase nutrient bioavailability and visual appeal
6. **Source quality ingredients** — prioritize raw nuts, fresh berries, and cold-pressed pomegranate juice for maximum polyphenol content
