## Core Concepts

**Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT)**
Pressurized chamber treatment delivering 100% oxygen through a mask at 2.0 ATA (atmospheres absolute) pressure. Works via hormesis—controlled oxidative stress triggers upregulation of the body's repair mechanisms, including enhanced angiogenesis, inflammation reduction, and cellular regeneration.

**PM2.5 Particulates**
Fine particles (2.5 micrometers) that bypass upper airway defenses and penetrate deep into lungs and potentially the bloodstream. Carries nitrogen oxides, sulfur dioxide, and carbon monoxide, causing cardiovascular damage, neurodegeneration, and respiratory disease.

**HBOT Skin**
The distinctive visible improvements in skin texture, tone, color, and radiance that result from HBOT—driven by increased collagen, elastin, and vascularization. Johnson describes it as superior to lasers, Botox, fillers, and other conventional skin treatments.

**Hormetic Response**
A biological adaptation where brief exposure to stress (in this case, high oxygen) triggers compensatory upregulation of protective systems. The key is pulsing the stimulus (oxygen on/off cycles) rather than constant exposure, allowing recovery and sustained benefit.

## Practical Takeaways

1. **Monitor Air Quality Daily**: Use IQ Air monitors or similar devices to track PM2.5 in your home and local area; treat this as a non-negotiable health system like sleep schedule.

2. **Optimize Home Air Filtration**: Upgrade HVAC filters to MERV 13 or above, add carbon filters for chemical pollutants, and use supplemental air purifiers (IQ Air, Dyson, Molekule) in main living spaces.

3. **Exercise Thresholds**: Avoid outdoor exercise above PM2.5 of 100 (ideally stay below 70); wear N95 masks above 150 PM2.5.

4. **HBOT Protocol for Longevity**: If pursuing HBOT, target 60 sessions in 90 days at 2.0 ATA pressure with 100% oxygen delivered via mask (not ambient chamber oxygen). Sessions should be 90 minutes with 20-minute oxygen pulses separated by 5-minute low-oxygen intervals at approximately 5 days per week.

5. **Support Antioxidant Defenses**: During high pollution exposure, consider N-acetylcysteine or glutathione supplementation to manage reactive oxygen species; use anti-inflammatory strategies (diet, supplements) to counter pollution-induced inflammation.

6. **Establish Health Systems**: Don't rely on in-the-moment decisions; commit to written plans for sleep, exercise timing, air quality checks, and dietary choices. Systematize health to reduce cognitive load and decision fatigue.

7. **Seek Reputable HBOT Providers**: Verify that facilities use hard-shell chambers (not soft-shell), deliver true 2.0 ATA pressure, provide 100% oxygen via mask, and use medical-grade oxygen cylinders (not concentrators that deliver 89-92% oxygen).

## Key Learnings & Conclusions

Hyperbaric oxygen therapy emerged as one of the most efficacious whole-body interventions Bryan Johnson has tested, with measurable improvements across inflammation, microbiome diversity, dementia risk, vascular function, and skin quality—driven by a hormetic mechanism that requires precise protocol adherence (pressure, oxygen purity, session duration, frequency). Simultaneously, his exposure to India's air pollution crisis revealed that environmental toxins pose a systemic, largely invisible health threat comparable to or exceeding cancer's lifespan impact, emphasizing that individual-level health optimization must be paired with awareness of environmental determinants that no personal protocol can fully overcome.
