Exposure to 10,000 lux light immediately upon waking signals the body that it's daytime, setting the internal clock that controls energy levels, alertness, and hormone production throughout the day.

**"Every Calorie Must Fight for Its Life"**
A philosophy where every food and nutrient consumed must demonstrate scientific evidence of efficacy and performance benefits; no empty calories or unproven supplements make it into the protocol.

**Biological Age Measurement**

**Pre-Sleep Digestion Window**

**Multi-Domain Resilience**
