This was one of the most important and difficult decisions I've ever made. I fired myself. To be fair, evening Bryan had a very tough job deciding what, when, and how much to eat during his daily shift from 5:00 p.m. to 10: p.m., when all the world's concerns were on his shoulders. 10 years of evening Bryan overeating led to gaining more than 50 lbs. This had devastating ripple effect effects on the other Bryans. The late night eating made quality sleep almost impossible for nighttime Bryan. and the lack of sleep made morning Bryan miserable and irritable evening Brian was making life miserable for all Bryans. So I fired him. He no longer has the authority to decide what he's going to eat. No more late night binges, no more second servings, no more 'just this once' rationalizations. Now, no matter what, only Blueprint Bryan can decide when, what, and how much to eat based upon data from all 70 organs of my body. This is now the new routine: breakfast at 7:00 a.m., lunch at 9:00, dinner at 11:00 a.m., fasting for 20 hours a day. The results have been spectacular. I have 60 less pounds to carry around and record levels of health and wellness. At a recent all-Bryan meeting, evening Bryan complained that sometimes he's hungry, but really he's just relieved from being the source of so much self-destruction. Nighttime Bryan now reliably gets restorative sleep, and morning Bryan is always well-rested and ready for the day. This is my Autonomous Self, demoting my conscious mind and opting into a system of wellness that is managed by data and science. The collective of Bryans has never been happier, and never more healthy.
