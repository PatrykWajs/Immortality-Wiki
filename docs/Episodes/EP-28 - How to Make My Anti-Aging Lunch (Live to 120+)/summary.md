## Overview

Bryan Johnson demonstrates how to prepare "Super Veggie," a nutrient-dense meal he eats daily as part of his multi-million dollar anti-aging protocol. The meal is designed with the philosophy that every calorie must "fight for its life," maximizing nutritional density while supporting longevity goals. The recipe can be prepared in 20 minutes and offers both whole-food and blended versions for different preferences and time constraints.

## Key Insights

- Bryan Johnson has invested millions of dollars over 3 years in slowing his aging speed through evidence-based dietary and lifestyle interventions
- Super Veggie is consumed daily and represents one of his favorite meals, indicating consistency is a cornerstone of his protocol
- The meal prioritizes vegetables with specific anti-aging compounds: cauliflower (150g) for anti-inflammatory effects and fiber, broccoli (250g) for antioxidants and bowel health, and mushrooms (50g) for immune system support
- Black lentils (150g) serve as the protein and fiber base, providing 25+ grams of plant-based protein per serving
- Spices like cumin (1 tbsp) and ginger (3g) are included for targeted organ health benefits including liver, pancreas, and artery support
- Apple cider vinegar (1 tbsp) is added specifically for blood sugar regulation
- Extra virgin olive oil (1 tbsp) is consumed with every meal for "incredible whole body benefits"
- The meal can take up to 34 minutes to eat in chopped form, or significantly less time in blended form

## Core Concepts

**Caloric Density vs. Nutritional Density**
Every ingredient must justify its place in the meal by providing specific anti-aging or health-promoting compounds, rather than empty calories.

**Plant-Based Protein Strategy**
Black lentils provide complete protein and fiber while keeping the meal plant-forward, supporting both longevity and digestive health.

**Targeted Ingredient Selection**
Each component addresses specific biological systems: anti-inflammatory pathways, blood sugar regulation, immune function, and cardiovascular health.

**Meal Preparation Flexibility**
Offering both whole-food and blended versions acknowledges real-world constraints while maintaining nutritional integrity.

## Practical Takeaways

1. Prepare Super Veggie weekly in batches using the exact measurements provided: 150g cauliflower, 250g broccoli, 50g mushrooms, 150g black lentils as the base
2. Add the functional ingredients: 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tsp hemp seeds, 3g ginger, 1 clove garlic, 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar, and 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
3. Boil broccoli, cauliflower, and mushrooms for 7-9 minutes until soft, then combine with lentil base
4. Choose between the whole-food version (up to 34 minutes to eat) or blended version (faster consumption) based on your schedule
5. Top with a sodium-free salt substitute if desired
6. Aim to incorporate this meal into your regular diet, ideally daily, as part of a comprehensive longevity protocol
7. Consider the 20-minute preparation time as an investment in your healthspan and lifespan
