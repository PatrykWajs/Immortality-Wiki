**Relationship Maturation** — The active process of investing in and developing a relationship over time, moving past surface-level attraction to genuine partnership and mutual growth.

- Actively seek relationship examples worth emulating rather than defaulting to societal scripts
- Communicate openly with your partner about whether the relationship is producing mutual joy and growth
- Be willing to do the hard work and show persistence—relationship building is not a quick process
