### [EP-8 — A typical Blueprint morning / Nov 2021](../Episodes/EP-8 - A typical Blueprint morning Nov 2021/summary.md)

### [EP-58 — How To Not Look Old](../Episodes/EP-58 - How To Not Look Old/summary.md)

The relationship with sun exposure requires nuanced balance rather than blanket avoidance or unlimited exposure. While sunlight is essential for vitamin D production, circadian rhythm regulation, and mood, excessive unprotected UV exposure accelerates skin aging and increases cancer risk. The optimal strategy combines timed sun exposure during low-UV windows, behavioral protections (physical barriers), supplementation when needed, and thoughtful sunscreen use with attention to evolving safety data. Longevity optimization in skin health depends on this integrated approach rather than single interventions.
